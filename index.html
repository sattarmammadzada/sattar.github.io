About

  Hello! I'm Sattar, a data enthusiast with a background in chemical engineering. After several years working in the chemical industry, I made the decision to transition to data analytics to fully utilize my problem-solving skills and enthusiasm for leveraging insights from data. Throughout my education and engineering career, I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking, statistical analysis, and process optimization, all of which I now apply to data science and analytics. .

  I have completed "Learn to code for data analysis – The Open University" and IBM Data Analytics courses. I’ve honed my skills in data cleaning, web scraping, and data manipulation using tools like Python, SQL, and Excel. In addition, I’ve gained experience working with data visualization tools like IBM Cognos Analytics and Power BI to communicate insights effectively to stakeholders .

  What excites me about data analytics is the ability to take raw data, uncover hidden patterns, and generate actionable insights that can drive business strategy. As I continue to build my expertise, I am eager to contribute to projects that push the boundaries of data analysis and help businesses harness the full potential of their data. If you're looking for someone who is detail-oriented, motivated, and enthusiastic about transforming data into valuable insights, feel free to connect with me.

Projects


Web Scraping

This project focuses on scraping data (price information) from online shop into Python dataframe and excel file. Price information gets updated daily to track changes.




Data Visualization with Python

In this project from IBM Data Analytics course, Python libraries - matplotlib, folium and seaborn were used to visualize historical automobile sales data to understand the historical trends in automobile sales during recession periods.




Data Analysis with Python

The House Sales Project analyzes a dataset containing house sale prices in King County, including Seattle, for properties sold between May 2014 and May 2015. The primary goal is to explore the factors influencing house prices and build predictive models.
